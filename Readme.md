# 🚀 DevDash — The Ultimate Developer Start Page

> **Open Source · Community Driven · Built by Developers, for Developers**

A lightweight, browser start page designed specifically for developers and professionals. It runs entirely from a local HTML file, giving you **total control** over your workspace with **100% privacy** — no servers, no tracking, no accounts.

We believe great tools should be free, open, and shaped by the community. **Contributions are welcome!** Fork the repo, submit a PR, or open an issue. Let's grow DevDash into a massive, feature-rich project together. 🌱

---

## ⚡ Key Features

1. **Dev Bookmarks:** Custom quick-links that automatically open in a **new tab**.
2. **Multi-Search Engine (v2+):** A built-in quick search bar supporting multiple search engines (all opening in new tabs).
3. **Multi-Slot Clipboard Cache:** Dedicated slots to save frequent usernames, app IDs, or text strings directly to your browser cache — better than OS history!
4. **Attractive Wallpapers:** Curated backgrounds to keep your workspace looking fresh.
5. **🕵️‍♂️ Hidden Secrets:** Built-in secret windows and easter eggs. *If you're smart, you'll find them.*
6. **JARVIS Welcome & Project Management:** Smart greeting, active project tracking, and multi-section bookmarks.
7. **Global Time Widgets:** Track time across multiple zones — essential for remote teams.
8. **Focus Timer:** Built-in Pomodoro-style timer to keep you in the zone.
9. **To-Do & Checklists:** Native task management with progress tracking.
10. **Seamless Data Export:** All your data can be exported to local JSON files — your data, your machine.

---

## ⚠️ Important Note: Backup Your Data!

> 🚨 **CRITICAL:** DevDash saves everything inside your browser's **LocalStorage**. If you clear your browser cookies/cache, your data will be wiped.
>
> **Always export and backup your Setup configuration as a JSON file** to ensure you never lose your data!
